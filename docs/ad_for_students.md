Agentic AI for Interactive Media Creation

The creation of Interactie Media (IM) require a series of closely interlinked skills, most importantly design skills, technical skills and writing skills (e.g. for producting content, documentation and report writing). The majority of creative practitioners (including students!) are *not* equally skilled and passionate across all three categories. As humans, we tend to specialise around what we love to do and where we feel our talent sits. As a consequence, specialised roles are commonly found in many IM production teams, including dedicated UX designers, digital artists, front-end developers, content producers, backend-developers, UX engineers and project managers. However, and increasing number of creators work independently - this applies to you as students doing your individual project or course work, but often presists into freelance mode of working in professional practice.

 I am working on an ad for students about this presentation - I've started the framing - the overall story: creative  
  media production requires many skills, of which you often only have a subset as an individual creator. Agentic AI    
  has proven ability to perform across all these areas - complementing individual creators; being code buddies; a      
  technical team member. This has transformative implications on how Interactive Media is produced, on how people      
  learn new skills, how they develop their careers, how they work in teams and how they create.
