# Design Guide

## Visual Direction

- **Minimalist and stylish** — clean layouts, generous whitespace, restrained color palette
- **Modern typography** — contemporary sans-serif or variable fonts (e.g., Inter, Space Grotesk, Satoshi, General Sans)
- **Subtle depth** — abstract shapes and generative patterns layered behind content with parallax scrolling to create a sense of spatial movement

## Background & Generative Elements

- Soft geometric shapes (circles, blobs, grids, lines)
- Low-opacity, blurred or semi-transparent
- Parallax movement tied to camera position on the canvas
- Should enhance, never distract from, the content

## Interaction & Motion

- Smooth easing for all camera transitions (ease-in-out or custom bezier)
- Focus expansion should feel fluid — scale up from position on canvas to full viewport
- Subtle hover/focus states for interactive elements
